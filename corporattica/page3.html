ICT Policy & Regulation                                       Page Three

Description:
Information and Communication Technologies policies and regulations continue to experience great change as governments and regulatory authorities search to increase work effectiveness, ensuring its support and fair services for consumers. Effective regulations based on decision-making and developing progressive policies are fundamental to any successful ICT program. Our company understands these components including international best practice in over 50 countries over 25 years for private sector clients, government agencies and regular authorities therefore, meeting World Trade Organization (WTO) and European Union Guidelines for developing country and emerging markets strategies.

Business Strategies:
LLTIC advises national governments on how they can take greater leadership in the creation of stable, pro-investment environments that facilitate greater ICT integration. Among the many interventions and services that can be identified for implementation are:
o Telecommunications sector reform, Policy & regulatory advice, Legal analysis, Economic analysis, Technology solutions for network developments, Financial/funding solutions, Professional training, and capacity building.

o Our company expert advisors are willing and able to help clients establish a demand and affordability models to enhance their businesses.

o We also advise and help in the drafting of legal contracts for licensing agreements, within the spectrum of local regulatory guidelines.

o LLTIC has an outstanding record of accomplishments when establishing rural developments funds, developing interconnection policy, rules, and guidance, for developing pro-competitive tariff policies and enforcement capabilities.

o Our company approach has a proven effectiveness in developing ICT policies and regulation is rooted in the design of customized solutions and in the considered implementation of these solutions through professional training, public relations, consultative, monitoring and enforcement actions.
